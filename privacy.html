Privacy Policy

At Hagzz, we understand the significance of handling your personal data responsibly and transparently. We are dedicated to protecting the information of all users who download and utilize our mobile application, Hagzz, in compliance with legal obligations and your rights. This Privacy Policy applies when we act as a data controller for processing the personal data of our mobile application users.

1. Definitions and Interpretation

In this Policy, the following terms have the following meanings:

  1. "Account" refers to an account necessary for accessing and/or using specific areas and features of our mobile application.
  2. "Cookie" indicates a small text file placed on your device by our mobile application when you visit certain parts or use certain features of our app. Details regarding Cookies are outlined in section 8 below.
  3. "Personal data" encompasses any data relating to an identifiable person who can be directly or indirectly identified from that data. This includes personal data provided by you through the mobile application.
  4. "We" or "us" or "our" pertains to Hagzz mobile application.
2. Your Rights

As a data subject, you have certain rights, which this Policy and our use of personal data aim to uphold:

  1. The right to be informed about our collection and use of personal data.
  2. The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  3. The right to rectification of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  4. The right to request deletion of personal data we hold about you.
  5. The right to restrict the processing of your personal data.
  6. The right to data portability.
  7. The right to object to the use of your personal data for specific purposes.
  8. Rights regarding automated decision-making and profiling.

If you believe that our processing of your personal information breaches data protection laws, you have the right to lodge a complaint. Please contact us using the details provided in section 9, and if necessary, you can also file a complaint with the Egyptian supervisory authority.

3. Date We Collect

Depending on your usage of the mobile application, we may collect some or all of the following personal and non-personal data. Please refer to section 8 for information on our use of Cookies and similar technologies:

  1. Name
  2. Birthdate
  3. Gender
  4. Favorite sports
  5. Location
  6. Contact information such as email addresses and telephone numbers
4. Use of Your Date

All personal data is processed and stored securely for no longer than necessary, and in accordance with GDPR regulations. Our use of your personal data is lawful and transparent, based on contractual necessity, consent, or legitimate interests. We may use your data for the following purposes:

  1. Providing and managing your Account
  2. Personalizing your experience and offering customized services based on your preferences.
  3. Showing products and services that are tailored to your needs.
  4. Analyzing your use of our mobile application and collecting feedback will help us to continually improve our services and enhance your experience.
  5. Responding to your inquiries
  6. Sending emails and messages related to services that you have opted for. You can always choose to opt out from any service through our mobile application.

Please note that third-party content on our mobile application may use third-party Cookies, and we advise reviewing their privacy policies for more information. Please see section 8 for more information about controlling Cookies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in section 4 and with your consent. We take appropriate measures to secure and protect your data, including ensuring secure connections and internal access controls.

6. Your Right to Withhold Information

You may access our mobile application without providing any data. However, certain features may require data submission. You also have the option to restrict our use of Cookies, as detailed in section 8.

7. How Can You Access Your Data?

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data held by us, free of charge. Please use the contact details in section 9..

8. Our Use of Cookies

Cookies are utilized to improve your mobile application experience and provide better services. We use both first-party and third-party Cookies for various purposes. Detailed information on Cookies and our Cookie Policy is available in section 8.

9. Contacting Us

For any questions regarding our mobile application or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info.hagzz@gmail.com

10. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically, with changes immediately posted on our mobile application. Your continued use of the mobile application after amendments indicates acceptance of the revised terms. We recommend checking this page regularly for updates.

The GDPR principles of data processing are adhered to, ensuring legality, fairness, transparency, purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, security, and accountability.
